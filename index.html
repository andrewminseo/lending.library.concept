Sustainability Lending Library

The Sustainability Lending Library is part of the eco-repping work that the Planet Blue Student Leaders are doing on the University of Michigan's Ann Arbor campus to create a more sustainable campus culture. This library operates as a resource sharing hub that provides common use equipment and materials for individual students and student groups.

Our mission is to cut down on excess waste, purchasing and consumption while creating a community space for the social practice of borrowing and sharing to take place.

Find Available Equipment

Looking for something specific? Browse our equipment categories below or check the availability calendar.

IMPORTANT: Please Read Before Requesting Items

  • Equipment requests must be submitted at least 5 business days (M-F, 9am-5pm) before the start of the desired rental date(s).
  • Screen printing requests must be submitted at least 10 business days (M-F, 9am-5pm) before the start of the desired rental date(s).
  • You are not guaranteed your desired rentals by filling out the request form. The Student Life Sustainability reserves the right to deny a request on an individual basis.
  • Rentals are on a "First Come, First Serve" basis and a reservation submitted before yours holds priority.
  • Check out each item's availability using our Google Calendar.
  • If any equipment is not returned or returned broken/damaged, your group/organization's shortcode will be charged with the cost of fixture or replacement.

Once your request form is submitted, a staff member from Student Life Sustainability will reach out with next steps on how and where to pick up materials.

How the Lending Library Works

Follow these simple steps to borrow equipment from our Sustainability Lending Library

1. Check Availability

Browse our equipment list and check availability on our Google Calendar

2. Submit Request Form

Fill out the online request form at least 5 business days before your event

3. Get Confirmation

A staff member will confirm your request and provide pickup details

4. Return Equipment

Return items in the same condition you received them by the agreed date
