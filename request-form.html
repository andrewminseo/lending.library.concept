Equipment Request Form

Fill out this form to request equipment from the Sustainability Lending Library. Please submit your request at least 5 business days before your event date.

IMPORTANT: Please Read Before Submitting

  • Equipment requests must be submitted at least 5 business days (M-F, 9am-5pm) before the start of the desired rental date(s).
  • Screen printing requests must be submitted at least 10 business days (M-F, 9am-5pm) before the start of the desired rental date(s).
  • You are not guaranteed your desired rentals by filling out this form. Student Life Sustainability reserves the right to deny a request on an individual basis.
  • Rentals are on a "First Come, First Serve" basis and a reservation submitted before yours holds priority.
  • If any equipment is not returned or returned broken/damaged, your group/organization's shortcode will be charged with the cost of fixture or replacement.

Once submitted, a staff member from Student Life Sustainability will reach out with next steps on how and where to pick up materials.
