Policies

Please review our policies regarding equipment borrowing, use, and return to ensure a smooth experience with the Sustainability Lending Library.

Eligibility

The Sustainability Lending Library is available to all University of Michigan community members, including:

  • Registered student organizations
  • Individual students with valid MCards
  • Faculty and staff members
  • Academic departments and programs

Priority is given to student organizations planning sustainable events or initiatives. All borrowers must present a valid MCard at the time of pickup.

Requesting Equipment

To ensure equipment availability and proper processing, all requests must adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Equipment requests must be submitted at least 5 business days (M-F, 9am-5pm) before the desired rental date.
  • Screen printing requests must be submitted at least 10 business days (M-F, 9am-5pm) before the desired rental date.
  • Requests are processed on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Submit requests through the official request form on our website.
  • Include detailed information about your event, including date, time, location, and purpose.
  • Student organizations must provide a valid shortcode.
IMPORTANT:

Submitting a request does not guarantee equipment availability. You will receive confirmation from a staff member if your request is approved.

Borrowing Period

Our standard borrowing periods are as follows:

  • Most equipment: 1-3 days
  • Special items (screen printing equipment, button maker): 1-2 days
  • Borrowing periods begin the day of pickup and end on the agreed-upon return date.

Extensions may be granted on a case-by-case basis if the equipment is not reserved by another user. To request an extension, contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled return date.

Pickup and Return

All equipment must be picked up and returned at our designated location:

Dana Building, Room 1046
440 Church Street
Ann Arbor, MI 48109

Pickup and return procedures:

  • Arrive at your scheduled pickup/return time.
  • Present your valid MCard for verification.
  • Inspect equipment at pickup and report any existing damage.
  • Return all equipment clean and in working condition.
Late Returns:

Late returns affect other users who may have reserved the equipment. Repeated late returns may result in temporary suspension of borrowing privileges.

Damage and Replacement

Borrowers are responsible for all equipment while in their possession. The following policies apply to damaged or lost items:

  • All equipment must be returned in the same condition it was borrowed.
  • Report any damage or malfunction immediately.
  • If equipment is damaged or lost, your group/organization's shortcode will be charged for the cost of repair or replacement.
  • Individual borrowers may be billed through their student account.
  • Failure to pay for damaged or lost items may result in loss of borrowing privileges.

We understand that accidents happen. If equipment is damaged while in your care, please be forthcoming about the issue so we can address it promptly.

Cancellations

If you need to cancel your reservation:

  • Notify us as soon as possible via email.
  • Include your name, organization, and the items you reserved.
  • Cancellations allow us to make the equipment available to others.

Repeated no-shows without notification may affect future borrowing privileges.

Usage Guidelines

To ensure the longevity of our equipment and availability for all users:

  • Use equipment only for its intended purpose.
  • Follow any provided instructions or guidelines.
  • Equipment should primarily be used for on-campus events and activities.
  • Off-campus use requires special approval and may be granted for university-sponsored events.
  • Do not alter, modify, or repair equipment yourself.
  • Clean equipment before returning it.

Borrower Agreement

By submitting a request to borrow equipment from the Sustainability Lending Library, you agree to:

  1. Take full responsibility for the care and safe return of all borrowed items.
  2. Return equipment by the agreed-upon date and time.
  3. Pay for any damages or loss that occurs while equipment is in your possession.
  4. Use the equipment for its intended purpose and in accordance with provided instructions.
  5. Notify the Sustainability Lending Library immediately if equipment is damaged, lost, or malfunctioning.
  6. Understand that failure to comply with these policies may result in the loss of borrowing privileges.

We appreciate your cooperation in helping us maintain our equipment in good condition for all members of the University of Michigan community to enjoy.
