How It Works

Learn about the borrowing process, timelines, and requirements for using the Sustainability Lending Library. Our goal is to make the sharing of resources simple and accessible for all University of Michigan students and organizations.

The Borrowing Process

1

Check Availability

Browse our equipment catalog and check the availability calendar to see if your desired items are available on your event date.

2

Submit Request

Fill out the request form with your contact information, organization details, and equipment needs at least 5 business days before your event.

3

Receive Confirmation

A Student Life Sustainability staff member will review your request and contact you with confirmation and pickup details.

4

Pick Up Equipment

Visit our location at the scheduled time to pick up your items. You'll need to present your MCard for verification.

5

Use Responsibly

Use the equipment for your event or project while taking good care of all borrowed items.

6

Return On Time

Return all items in good condition by the agreed-upon return date to avoid late fees or replacement charges.

Who Can Borrow?

The Sustainability Lending Library is available to all members of the University of Michigan community, with priority given to:

  • Registered student organizations
  • Student project teams
  • Academic departments and classes
  • Individual students with valid MCards
  • Faculty and staff planning sustainable events

Borrowing Requirements

  • Valid MCard for verification
  • Organization shortcode (for registered student orgs)
  • Approval of request by Student Life Sustainability
  • Agreement to return items in good condition
  • Understanding that you are responsible for any damages

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if equipment is damaged?

If equipment is damaged during your use, your group/organization's shortcode will be charged for the cost of repair or replacement. Individual borrowers may be billed through their student account. It's important to report any pre-existing damage at pickup.

Can I extend my borrowing period?

Extensions may be granted on a case-by-case basis. Contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled return date to request an extension. Approval depends on whether the items are reserved by another group.

Is there a limit to how many items I can borrow?

While there's no strict limit, requests are evaluated based on availability and necessity. For large events requiring multiple items, we recommend submitting requests well in advance.

Can I reserve equipment for recurring events?

Yes, you can request equipment for recurring events. Please specify all dates in your request form. For semester-long reservations, special approval may be required.

What if I need to cancel my reservation?

Please notify us as soon as possible if you need to cancel. This allows us to make the equipment available to others. Repeated no-shows without notification may affect future borrowing privileges.

Ready to borrow equipment?

Check our equipment catalog, then submit a request form to get started with your sustainable event or project.
