Request Submitted Successfully!

Thank you for submitting your equipment request to the Sustainability Lending Library. We've received your submission and will review it shortly.

What Happens Next?

  1. A staff member from Student Life Sustainability will review your request within 1-2 business days.
  2. You'll receive a confirmation email with pickup details and any additional information.
  3. Pick up your requested equipment at the designated time and location.
  4. Use the equipment for your event or project.
  5. Return all items in good condition by the agreed-upon return date.

If you have any questions or need to modify your request, please email us at planetbluestudentleader@umich.edu.
