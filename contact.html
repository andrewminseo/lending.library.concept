Additional Information

Please bring your MCard when picking up equipment. For large or heavy items, we recommend bringing a cart or additional help for transportation.

If you have special requirements or need accommodations for pickup or return, please mention this in your request form or contact us directly.

Parking is available in the Church Street parking structure (fee required) or at metered spaces on nearby streets.
