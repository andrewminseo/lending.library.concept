Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Sustainability Lending Library. If you don't see your question here, feel free to contact us directly.

General Questions

What is the Sustainability Lending Library?

The Sustainability Lending Library is a resource-sharing hub run by the Planet Blue Student Leaders at the University of Michigan. We provide common-use equipment and materials for individual students and student groups to borrow, with the goal of reducing excess waste, purchasing, and consumption on campus.

Who can use the Lending Library?

The Sustainability Lending Library is available to all University of Michigan students, registered student organizations, faculty, and staff members with a valid MCard. Priority is given to student organizations planning sustainable events or initiatives.

Is there a fee to borrow items?

No, all equipment is available to borrow free of charge. However, your group/organization's shortcode or personal student account will be charged for any damaged or unreturned items.

Where is the Lending Library located?

Our main location is in the Dana Building, Room 1046, at 440 Church Street, Ann Arbor, MI 48109. All equipment pickup and returns must be scheduled in advance.

Borrowing Process

How do I request equipment?

To request equipment, fill out the request form on our website at least 5 business days before your desired rental date. The form requires your contact information, organization details, and a list of items you'd like to borrow.

How far in advance should I submit my request?

Regular equipment requests must be submitted at least 5 business days before your desired rental date. Screen printing requests require 10 business days' notice. For large events or if you need multiple items, we recommend submitting your request even earlier.

How long can I borrow equipment?

The standard borrowing period is 1-3 days, depending on the item and demand. For special circumstances or longer events, you can request an extended borrowing period, which we'll approve on a case-by-case basis.

What do I need to bring when picking up equipment?

When picking up equipment, bring your valid MCard for identification. For large or heavy items, we recommend bringing a cart or additional help for transportation.

Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation by emailing us as soon as possible. We appreciate advance notice so we can make the equipment available to others. Repeated no-shows without notification may affect future borrowing privileges.

Equipment Questions

What types of equipment do you offer?

We offer a wide range of equipment, including event supplies (tables, tents, sandwich boards), technology items (speakers, HDMI cords, megaphones), craft supplies (screen printing equipment, button makers), and sustainability tools (energy meters). Browse our complete inventory on the Equipment page.

How do I check if an item is available?

You can check item availability by viewing our Google Calendar, which shows when items are reserved. The calendar link is available on our website. You can also contact us directly to inquire about specific items.

Do you provide instructions for using equipment?

Yes, basic usage instructions are provided for specialized equipment when you pick it up. For items like screen printing equipment or the button maker, we can provide more detailed guidance if needed.

Can I suggest new items for the library?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for new equipment to add to our inventory. You can suggest items on the request form or contact us directly with your ideas.

Policies

What happens if equipment is damaged?

If equipment is damaged during your use, your group/organization's shortcode will be charged for the cost of repair or replacement. Individual borrowers may be billed through their student account. It's important to report any pre-existing damage at pickup.

What if I return items late?

Late returns affect other users who may have reserved the equipment. Repeated late returns may result in temporary suspension of borrowing privileges. If you need an extension, contact us before your return date to request one.

Can I reserve equipment for recurring events?

Yes, you can request equipment for recurring events by specifying all dates in your request form. For semester-long reservations, special approval may be required and will depend on demand for the requested items.

Can someone else pick up or return items on my behalf?

Yes, but you must notify us in advance with the name of the person who will be picking up or returning items on your behalf. That person must bring their MCard for identification. The original borrower remains responsible for the equipment.

Can I take equipment off campus?

In general, equipment should be used for on-campus events and activities. Off-campus use requires special approval and may be granted for university-sponsored events. Please specify this need in your request form.
